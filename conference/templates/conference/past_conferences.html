NCPS 2023 – Goa, India

The National Conference on Polar Sciences 2023 was held in Goa and brought together scientists, academicians, and early-career researchers working across polar science, oceanography, and cryosphere research.

Conference Highlights
  • In-person scientific deliberations and interdisciplinary discussions
  • Keynote lectures by leading national experts in polar and ocean sciences
  • Technical and poster sessions showcasing recent research outcomes
Scientific Focus Areas
  • Climate Change & Polar Variability
  • Cryospheric Processes & Glacier Dynamics
  • Southern Ocean Circulation & Biogeochemistry
  • Himalayan Cryosphere & Water Resources
  • Polar Technology, Remote Sensing & Modelling
Participation & Representation
  • Mode: Physical
  • Participants: 250+
  • Institutions: Universities, research institutes, government organisations
Conference Outcomes

NCPS 2023 strengthened interdisciplinary collaboration, promoted early-career researcher participation, and contributed to India’s evolving polar and ocean research roadmap.

NCPS 2021 – Virtual Conference

NCPS 2021 was conducted in a fully virtual format, enabling wide participation from national and international researchers during global travel restrictions.

Conference Highlights
  • Virtual keynote lectures and thematic technical sessions
  • Online poster presentations and interactive discussions
  • Broad national and international participation
Scientific Focus Areas
  • Polar Climate Systems & Variability
  • Sea Ice & Cryosphere–Atmosphere Interactions
  • Southern Ocean Processes
  • Remote Sensing & Polar Observations
Participation & Representation
  • Mode: Virtual
  • Participants: 300+
  • Institutions: National & international research organisations
Conference Outcomes

NCPS 2021 ensured continuity of scientific exchange during the pandemic and facilitated collaborative dialogue across polar, ocean, and cryosphere research domains.
