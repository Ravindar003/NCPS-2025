Welcome to NCPS 2027

Advancing Polar & Ocean Sciences for Societal Services

January 16 – 18, 2027 Goa, India

The National Conference on Polar Sciences (NCPS 2027) focuses on delivering practical, science-based insights for climate resilience, sustainable oceans, cryospheric research, and environmental security. The conference provides a multidisciplinary platform for researchers, policymakers, and practitioners to exchange knowledge and advance India’s polar and ocean science missions.

Venue

The conference will be held at one of India’s premier coastal research locations

National Centre for Polar and Ocean Research

Goa, India

The National Conference on Polar Sciences (NCPS 2027) will be hosted at the National Centre for Polar and Ocean Research, located in Goa. The venue offers world-class research infrastructure, conference facilities, and a unique coastal environment that supports interdisciplinary scientific exchange.

  • Headland Sada, Vasco-da-Gama, Goa
  • Premier Government Research Institution
  • Well connected by air, rail, and road

Accommodation

Suggested stay options in Vasco-da-Gama, Goa

To ensure a comfortable and convenient stay for participants attending the National Conference on Polar Sciences (NCPS), we have arranged a selection of quality accommodation options in Vasco-da-Gama, Goa, just minutes away from the National Centre for Polar and Ocean Research (NCPOR) – the conference venue.

Whether you prefer a business-class hotel, a budget-friendly option, or a scenic beachside property, Vasco city offers accommodation to suit every preference. We encourage early reservations to secure your stay, as rooms will be allotted on a first-come, first-served basis.

All Suggested hotels are located in proximity to the venue and are easily accessible by local transport or taxi services. Vasco-da-Gama city also offers convenient connectivity to Goa International Airport (Dabolim), which is approximately 5 km from NCPOR. Restaurants, cafes, and shops are located nearby, ensuring that participants have access to all amenities during their visit.

Suggested Hotels

  • The HQ approx. 3.5 km from NCPOR
  • Hotel Supreme approx. 4 km from NCPOR
  • The Flora Grand approx. 5 km from NCPOR
  • La-Paz Gardens Beacon approx. 3.5 km from NCPOR
