Frequently Asked Questions

This page will be updated with commonly asked questions about registration, abstract submission, and participation.

NCPS 2025 is open to researchers, students, faculty, industry professionals, and policymakers working in polar and ocean sciences.

Use the registration page to create an account. After logging in, you can access your dashboard and submit abstracts.

After login, go to Dashboard → “View / Submit” under Your Submissions, then follow the submission form instructions.

The deadline is shown on your dashboard and on the homepage under Important Dates.

You can download it from the Brochure page (Quick Links → Download Brochure).
