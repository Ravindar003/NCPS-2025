## Abstract Submission Guidelines

### Submission Process
1. **Login**: First register and login to your account
2. **Navigate**: Go to Dashboard → Submit Abstract
3. **Fill Details**:
   - Title of research
   - List of authors (main author and co-authors)
   - Institutional affiliations
   - Keywords (4-6 keywords)
   - Select scientific theme
4. **Write Abstract**: 250-300 words
5. **Upload**: Submit abstract in required format
6. **Submit**: Review and submit for peer review

### Abstract Format Requirements
- **Length**: 250-300 words
- **Structure**: 
  - Introduction/Background
  - Methodology
  - Results/Findings
  - Conclusion
- **Font**: Times New Roman, 11pt
- **Line Spacing**: 1.5
- **Keywords**: 4-6 relevant keywords
- **File Format**: PDF or Word document

### Review Process
1. All abstracts undergo peer review by scientific committee
2. Evaluation based on:
   - Scientific merit and originality
   - Relevance to conference themes
   - Clarity of presentation
   - Methodological rigor
3. Authors notified within 2-3 weeks
4. Possible outcomes: Accept, Reject, Revise and Resubmit

### After Acceptance
- Authors will be notified of presentation format (oral or poster)
- Upload final revised abstract if requested
- Prepare presentation as per guidelines

## Presentation Guidelines

### Oral Presentations
- **Duration**: 15 minutes presentation + 5 minutes Q&A (Total: 20 minutes)
- **Format**: PowerPoint or PDF
- **Screen**: 16:9 aspect ratio recommended
- **Content**:
  - Title slide with authors and affiliations
  - Clear objectives and methodology
  - Key results with visuals
  - Conclusions and future work
- **Tips**:
  - Use large fonts (minimum 24pt for text)
  - Limit text on slides
  - Use high-quality images and graphs
  - Practice timing

### Poster Presentations
- **Size**: A0 (841mm × 1189mm)
- **Orientation**: Portrait (vertical)
- **Layout**:
  - Title at top (bold, large font)
  - Authors and affiliations
  - Introduction, Methods, Results, Conclusions sections
  - References
- **Design Tips**:
  - Use clear headings
  - Include visuals (charts, photos, diagrams)
  - Keep text concise and readable from 1-2 meters
  - Use institutional/project logos if applicable
- **Display**: Poster boards and pins will be provided

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Registration FAQs

**Q: Is registration mandatory?**
A: Yes, all participants must register to attend the conference.

**Q: What is the registration fee?**
A: Fee structure will be announced soon. Students typically get discounted rates.

**Q: Can I register without submitting an abstract?**
A: Yes, you can attend as a participant without presenting.

**Q: How do I know if my registration is confirmed?**
A: You'll receive a confirmation email after admin approval.

### Abstract Submission FAQs

**Q: Can I submit multiple abstracts?**
A: Yes, you can submit multiple abstracts on different topics.

**Q: Can I change my abstract after submission?**
A: You can edit until the deadline. After review, only if revision is requested.

**Q: What if my abstract is rejected?**
A: You can still attend as a participant. Reviewers may suggest improvements.

**Q: How do I know if it's oral or poster?**
A: The acceptance notification will specify the presentation format.

**Q: Can I upload supplementary materials?**
A: The abstract submission form allows file uploads.

### Technical FAQs

**Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**
A: Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.

**Q: The website is not loading properly.**
A: Try clearing browser cache or use a different browser (Chrome, Firefox recommended).

**Q: I'm having trouble uploading files.**
A: Ensure file size is under 5MB and format is PDF or Word.

**Q: Can I use the chatbot for technical support?**
A: Yes! Ask me about any issues and I'll help or direct you to support.

### Conference FAQs

**Q: Will presentations be recorded?**
A: Details about recording will be announced closer to the conference.

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
A: Check the website for updates on conference app availability.

**Q: What COVID-19 protocols will be in place?**
A: Safety guidelines will be shared with registered participants.

**Q: Can I bring guests?**
A: Conference is for registered participants only. Check with organizers for special cases.
